Meet Dencity.
Middle Housing
Made Easy

With Dencity, middle housing planning and development has never been easier. Using up-to-date Regional Housing and Geographic information, Dencity matches pre-approved plans with your sites requirements, and enables rapid ideation of ideas.

Use Dencity to Tailor the Perfect Solution in Minutes

Whether you currently own a home or are planning to, Dencity is a intuitive tool that enables you to explore development options as an independent actor. Expanding upon frameworks established with ADUniverse, a portal built by the Seattle Office of Planning and Community Development and Department of Construction, Dencity expands the scope of ready-made housing plans to encourage development.

Build with Confidence, and Rapidly Ideate Project Ideas

Dencity is built with Developers in mind, housing up-to-date housing ordinance and planning policies in both Seattle and greater King County. Utilize Dencity to scope out potential developments, and identify high yeild locations. If you're looking to get your housing plan approved by the Seattle Department of Construction and Inspections, Dencity will help make it happen.
